Teaspoon Of Boba

Teaspoon of Boba is drinks stand that sells the popular Taiwanese drink of bubble tea. A delicious refreshing and filling beverage that has sweet, flavored tea combined with milk and chewy tapioca pearls, creating a unique and enjoyable drink experience.

Our Collaborators

Teaspoon Of Boba has collaborated with various businesses and people within the state. Our collaboration drinks are a way of repressenting the community we are apart of, spreading support and appriciation for all the other hard working memebers of the island.
We have collaborated with the likes of SAFR, Bento, BigSeoulLTD, Dreamcore and more.
